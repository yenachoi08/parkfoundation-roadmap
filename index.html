The Selection & Implementation Lifecycle
Phase 1: Needs Discovery
"De-risking the Investment"
Requirements Mapping
Interviewing Program Officers to turn current "manual workarounds" into a must-have technical feature list.
Institutional Knowledge Audit
Identifying mission-critical data living outside the current system to ensure it's captured in the new one.
Financial Integration Prep
Mapping touchpoints with Sage Intacct to ensure the new GMS eliminates redundant double-entry.
Phase 2: Vetting & Migration
"The Technical Heavy Lifting"
Vendor Scorecarding
Objective comparison of Fluxx, Temelio, and GivingData against our specific 8 program area needs.
The "Data Scrub"
Cleaning duplicate grantee records and missing compliance docs *before* migration to ensure a fresh start.
Automated Compliance
Configuring hard-stop gates for IRS status and due diligence directly into the new workflow.
Phase 3: Adoption & ROI
"Earned Confidence"
The Sandbox Period
A safe environment where staff can practice the new system without fear of affecting live grant data.
Custom SOP Library
Video tutorials and guides built specifically for Park’s workflows, not generic vendor manuals.
Optimization Cadence
Monthly "clinics" to refine settings based on real-world use, ensuring the system evolves with the mission.
Strategic Value to the Leadership Team
For the CFO

A system that integrates with finance to provide governance-grade data and removes the audit risk of manual data re-entry.

For Program Staff

Automation of administrative tasks (reminders, letters) that frees up hours each week to focus on grantee relationships.

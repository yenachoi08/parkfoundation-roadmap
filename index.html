A few things I’ve learned along the way
🤝Reducing the Burden of Process
Technology should absorb administrative labor so people don’t have to. By automating chores like the LOI-to-proposal bridge, we return time to partners for their mission.
⚖️Integrity through Validation
Technical transitions succeed when they earn trust. We prioritize a proactive data scrub followed by parallel testing periods to ensure seamless grantmaking.

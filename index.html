The leadership philosophy behind this roadmap

This approach moves beyond "what GMS should we buy" and focuses on the organizational trust and process clarity required to make any technology succeed at Park Foundation. Foundations that struggle with digital tools don't have a software problem — they have a process and confidence problem. This roadmap resolves both before any system goes live.

01
People before platforms
System champions — like Heidi and Amy — are identified in Phase 1 so technology solves real pain points, not assumed ones.
02
Data integrity as a prerequisite
No migration, no configuration until the underlying data is clean. Dirty data in a new system is worse than dirty data in the old one.
03
Compliance as architecture
Hard-stop gates embed compliance into the workflow — protecting Roberta's audit readiness without adding manual oversight burden.
04
Continuous improvement
Monthly clinics mean the system grows with Park Foundation's needs — it reflects the team's reality, not a vendor's template.

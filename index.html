Guiding Principles for Implementation
Center the Grantee Experience Trust-based philanthropy requires minimizing applicant burden. By automating the technical "heavy lifting"—like the LOI-to-proposal bridge—we return time to our partners for their mission, not our paperwork.
Tech as a Silent Partner The goal is a system that works for the team, not one they work for. By automating compliance "chores" like IRS verification and docket prep, we protect the team’s mental bandwidth for relational grantmaking.
Data as a Relational Mirror A database should remember partners as well as staff does. Cleaning legacy data ensures returning partners are greeted by a system that recognizes their long history and value with the foundation.
